# Ethanol Fill Checklist

*Role: Fills fuel into transfer tank, monitors fill level, and disconnects QD.*

## Setup
- [ ] **DON PPE** (Gloves, Safety goggles, close-toed shoes, long pants).
- [ ] Inspect transfer tank for cleanliness.
- [ ] Verify funnel and measurement tools are clean and dry.
- [ ] Lay out materials for fuel transfer in stable position.
- [ ] Confirm spill containment materials are nearby.
- [ ] **TELL PAD MANAGER / GSE MANAGER ETHANOL FUEL FILL PREP IS READY.**

## Dry Run & Testing
- [ ] Review the planned fuel volume and target tank conditions with Operations Manager and Mission Control Manager.
- [ ] Walk through a verbal dry run of the fill sequence (valve order, QD connect/disconnect, clear zone) with Pad Manager.
- [ ] Confirm you understand the abort phrase and who to call if a spill or leak occurs.

## Press Proof
- [ ] Confirm with GSE Manager that low-pressure GSE will provide the correct pressurization setpoint for Fuel Fill.
- [ ] Confirm with Mission Control Manager that telemetry for fuel tank pressure is available and nominal.

## Fill
- [ ] **BREAK HERE. WAIT FOR OPERATIONS MANAGER TO GIVE THE GO TO COMMENCE FUEL FILL.**
- [ ] Confirm system is **ARMED** via Mission Control.
- [ ] Confirm clear zone around fuel transfer area.
- [ ] Connect Fuel Fill Line between Fuel Tank and Transfer Tank.
- [ ] Confirm QD is properly seated and locked.
- [ ] Open Fuel Fill Valve.
- [ ] Open Fuel Transfer Tank Vent.
- [ ] Open Fuel Transfer Tank bottom ball valve.
- [ ] Pour measured fuel volume (90% of jug liquid) into transfer tank using funnel.
- [ ] Monitor for leaks at fittings and QDs during transfer.
- [ ] Close Fuel Fill Valve.
- [ ] Close Fuel Transfer Tank Vent.
- [ ] Confirm no visible leaks or drips.
- [ ] Command AND confirm transition to Fuel Fill state.
- [ ] Transfer tank will receive **150 PSIG** from LOW PRESS GSE.
- [ ] Monitor tank pressure via telemetry.
- [ ] Monitor fill level behavior and vent response.
- [ ] **BREAK HERE. WAIT FOR PRESSURIZATION FROM MISSION CONTROL.**
- [ ] Monitor tank vent for gas indicator signaling full tank (venting from Fuel Tank).
- [ ] Immediately close fuel fill transfer tank bottom ball valve.
- [ ] Command Mission Control to return to **ARMED** state.
- [ ] Verify Mission Control returns system to **ARMED** state.
- [ ] Confirm pressure stabilizes at expected value.
- [ ] Repeat preparation steps.
- [ ] Confirm system is **ARMED** via Mission Control.
- [ ] Confirm clear zone around fuel transfer area.
- [ ] Open Fuel Fill Valve.
- [ ] Open Fuel Transfer Tank Vent.
- [ ] Pour measured fuel volume (10% of jug liquid) into transfer tank using funnel.
- [ ] Monitor for leaks at fittings and QDs during transfer.
- [ ] Close Fuel Fill Valve.
- [ ] Close Fuel Transfer Tank Vent.
- [ ] Open Fuel Transfer Tank bottom ball valve.
- [ ] Confirm no visible leaks or drips.
- [ ] Command AND confirm transition to Fuel Fill state.
- [ ] Transfer tank will receive **150 PSIG** from LOW PRESS GSE.
- [ ] Monitor tank pressure via telemetry.
- [ ] Monitor fill level behavior and vent response.
- [ ] **BREAK HERE. WAIT FOR PRESSURIZATION FROM MISSION CONTROL.**
- [ ] Monitor tank vent for gas indicator signaling full tank (venting from Fuel Tank).
- [ ] Unplug fuel fill QD and command Operations Manager to return to **ARMED** state.
- [ ] Verify Mission Control returns system to **ARMED** state.
- [ ] Confirm pressure stabilizes at expected value.
- [ ] Inspect QD and fittings for leaks after disconnect.
- [ ] Open fuel transfer tank dump valve to ensure fuel was fully transferred.
- [ ] **TELL PAD MANAGER FUEL FILL IS COMPLETE AND QD IS UNPLUGGED.**
- [ ] **TELL MISSION CONTROL MANAGER AND OPERATIONS MANAGER THAT FUEL FILL IS COMPLETE AND TANK PRESSURE IS STABLE AT EXPECTED VALUE.**

## Hotfire
- [ ] **WAIT FOR MISSION CONTROL MANAGER TO PROCEED TO FIRE.**
