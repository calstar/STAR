# Ethanol Fill Checklist

*Role: Fills fuel into transfer tank, monitors fill level, and disconnects QD.*

## Preparation
- [ ] **DON PPE** (Safety goggles, close-toed shoes, long pants).
- [ ] Inspect transfer tank for cleanliness and proper vent routing.
- [ ] Verify funnel and measurement tools are clean and dry.
- [ ] Lay out materials for fuel transfer in stable position.
- [ ] Confirm spill containment materials are nearby.
- [ ] **TELL PAD MANAGER / GSE MANAGER ETHANOL FUEL FILL PREP IS READY.**

## Fuel Fill Execution
- [ ] **BREAK HERE. WAIT FOR OPERATIONS MANAGER TO GIVE THE GO TO COMMENCE FUEL FILL.**
- [ ] Confirm system is **ARMED** via Mission Control.
- [ ] Confirm clear zone around fuel transfer area.
- [ ] Connect Fuel Fill Line between Fuel Tank and Transfer Tank.
- [ ] Confirm QD is properly seated and locked.
- [ ] Open Fuel Fill Valve.
- [ ] Open Fuel Transfer Tank Vent.
- [ ] Pour measured fuel volume (e.g., **10 Liters standard**) into transfer tank using funnel.
- [ ] Monitor for leaks at fittings and QDs during transfer.
- [ ] Close Fuel Fill Valve.
- [ ] Close Fuel Transfer Tank Vent.
- [ ] Confirm no visible leaks or drips.
- [ ] Command or confirm transition to Fuel Fill state.
- [ ] Pressurize tank to **400 PSIG** per procedure.
- [ ] Monitor tank pressure rise via telemetry.
- [ ] Monitor fill level behavior and vent response.

## Pressurization & Conclusion
- [ ] **BREAK HERE. WAIT FOR PRESSURIZATION FROM MISSION CONTROL.**
- [ ] Monitor tank vent for gas indicator signaling full tank (venting from Fuel Tank).
- [ ] Confirm pressure stabilizes at expected value.
- [ ] Verify Mission Control returns system to **ARMED** state.
- [ ] Confirm pressure stable before disconnecting.
- [ ] Unplug QD safely.
- [ ] Inspect QD and fittings for leaks after disconnect.
- [ ] **TELL PAD MANAGER FUEL FILL IS COMPLETE AND QD IS UNPLUGGED.**
