# Ethanol Fill Checklist

*Role: Fills fuel into transfer tank, monitors fill level in tank, and unplugs QD.*

## Preparation
- [ ] **DON PPE** (Safety goggles, close-toed shoes, pants).
- [ ] Lay out materials for fuel transfer.
- [ ] **TELL PAD MANAGER / GSE MANAGER ETHANOL FUEL FILL PREP IS READY.**

## Fuel Fill Execution
- [ ] **BREAK HERE. WAIT FOR OPERATIONS MANAGER TO GIVE THE GO TO COMMENCE FUEL FILL.**
- [ ] Ensure system is ARMED via Mission Control.
- [ ] Connect Fuel Fill Line between Fuel Tank and Transfer Tank.
- [ ] Open Fuel Fill Valve and Fuel Transfer Tank Vent.
- [ ] Fill fuel into the transfer tank (e.g., 10 Liters standard) using a funnel.
- [ ] Close Fuel Fill Valve and Transfer Tank Vent.
- [ ] Go to Fuel Fill state and pressurize the tank to **400 PSIG** per procedure.
- [ ] Monitor fill level securely in the tank.

## Pressurization & Conclusion
- [ ] **BREAK HERE. WAIT FOR PRESSURIZATION FROM MISSION CONTROL.**
- [ ] Monitor tank vent for fuel gas indicator signalling full tank (venting gas from Fuel Tank).
- [ ] Unplug QD once fill is confirmed and system is returned to ARMED state.
- [ ] **TELL PAD MANAGER FUEL FILL IS COMPLETE AND QD IS UNPLUGGED.**
