# VIDCOMM (Video) Checklist

*Role: Ensure video cameras are setup and connected to server properly. Work with mission control to confirm angles.*

## Camera Setup
- [ ] Setup camera monitoring stations focusing on pad and fluid lines.
- [ ] Ensure cameras are connected to the central server.
- [ ] Establish communication with Mission Control Manager.
- [ ] **TELL MISSION CONTROL MANAGER TO CONFIRM CAMERA ANGLES.**
- [ ] Check camera batteries, weatherproofing, and power sources.
