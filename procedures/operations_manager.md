# Operations Manager Checklist

*Role: Main safety manager. Enforces checklists and procedures. Primary POC with FAR range staff.*

## Initial Safety Verification
- [ ] Confirm all personnel are wearing required PPE per role.
- [ ] Confirm Safety Officer and Pad Manager are present and ready.
- [ ] Verify emergency response plan is understood by all key personnel.
- [ ] Establish initial POC with FAR range staff.
- [ ] **BREAK HERE. WAIT FOR ALL PERSONNEL TO BE IN DESIGNATED SAFE AREAS AND PAD SAFETY OFFICER CHECKOFFS TO COMPLETE.**

## Procedures Oversight
- [ ] Give official GO to Pad Manager to commence Pad Setup.
- [ ] Confirm Safety Officer checkoffs completed.
- [ ] Coordinate timing with Mission Control Manager and Pad Manager.
- [ ] Ensure checklists are followed in written order without deviation.
- [ ] Require verbal confirmations before critical transitions.
- [ ] Prioritize safety checks before:
  - [ ] Fuel Fill
  - [ ] Cryogen Fill
  - [ ] Pressurization
  - [ ] Ignition
- [ ] Confirm clear zone and safe LOS before fluid fills.
- [ ] **TELL MISSION CONTROL / PAD MANAGERS TO PROCEED WITH FLUID FILLS UPON CONFIRMATION OF CLEAR ZONE (PER MASTER PROCEDURE).**

## Pressurization & Hotfire Attempt
- [ ] Confirm safe distance protocol enforced.
- [ ] Confirm all non-essential personnel are outside hazard boundary.
- [ ] Standby for Mission Control verification of remote pressurization.
- [ ] Monitor for any deviation from expected procedure flow.
- [ ] **EXECUTE ABORT COMMUNICATION IMMEDIATELY IF SAFETY IS COMPROMISED.**
- [ ] Maintain real-time coordination with FAR range staff during pressurization and burn.
- [ ] Confirm ignition authorization only after all safety criteria are satisfied.

## Post-Test / Debrief
- [ ] Confirm system safing parameters met (< 25 psi confirmed by Mission Control).
- [ ] Authorize personnel return to pad only after safing confirmation.
- [ ] Report conclusion of firing to FAR range staff.
- [ ] Document procedural deviations or safety concerns.
