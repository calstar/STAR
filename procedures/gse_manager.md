# GSE Manager Checklist

*Role: Operator in charge of Ground Support Equipment (fill, pressurization, actuation — NOT electronics). Works with ECOMM/ACTCOMM to ensure sensors and actuators connect properly.*

## Setup
- [ ] Layout materials and wheel out Ground Support Equipment (GSE) system.
- [ ] Position GSE in designated safe operating location.
- [ ] Ensure regulators are installed in correct orientation (HP to HP, LP to LP).
- [ ] Verify non-electrical valves are mounted per P&ID.
- [ ] Inspect hose routing for kinks, abrasion, or stress points.
- [ ] Verify GSE-side hoses, pipes, and any harnessing are routed and supported per plan (no kinks or abrasion, not across walk paths, clear of vents and moving hardware).
- [ ] Check fluid fitting tightness throughout GSE system (do not overtighten).
- [ ] Check actuator mounts for alignment and slop.
- [ ] Confirm NPT fittings have proper thread sealant application/taped properly.
- [ ] Ensure that low press GSE is connected to low press K-bottle. Ensure bottle fitting is **TIGHT**.
- [ ] Confirm low press GSE vent is closed. 
- [ ] Open low press K-bottle.
- [ ] Communicate to Mission Control Manager that low press is ready.
- [ ] **TELL ACTCOMM TO VERIFY PNEUMATIC ACTUATORS.**
- [ ] **TELL ECOMM TO VERIFY SENSOR CONNECTIONS ON GSE.**
- [ ] Confirm solenoid manifold actuator mapping matches P&ID.
- [ ] Confirm pneumatic supply lines are labeled and correctly routed.
- [ ] **BREAK HERE. WAIT FOR ACTCOMM/ECOMM TO CONFIRM CONNECTIONS.**
- [ ] Ensure pressurized bottles are secure (strapped and stable).
- [ ] Attach pressure and fill lines from K-bottle to pressure side GSEs (high side and low side).
- [ ] Ensure that the fuel fill line is connected to the fuel fill GSE.
- [ ] Ensure the LOX fill line is connected to the dewar.
- [ ] Confirm QDs are properly seated and locked.
- [ ] Confirm vent lines are routed away from personnel and equipment.
- [ ] Set system and GSE fluid valves to safe starting state.
- [ ] **TELL PAD MANAGER GSE IS READY FOR PRESSURIZATION HAZARD CHECK/SAFETY OFFICER CHECKOFFS.**

## Dry Run & Testing
- [ ] Coordinate with ACTCOMM and ECOMM on which actuations and sensor checks will be performed during dry run.
- [ ] Observe GSE-side actuations during dry run to confirm:
  - [ ] Correct solenoids and valves move for each commanded state.
  - [ ] No hoses or fittings shift into unsafe positions.
  - [ ] No leaks appear during cycling.
- [ ] Confirm with ACTCOMM that pneumatic actuation is repeatable and timing is nominal.
- [ ] Confirm with ECOMM that GSE sensor telemetry (pressures, any temps) matches expected values during dry run states.
- [ ] **TELL PAD MANAGER AND MISSION CONTROL MANAGER WHETHER GSE IS GO OR NO-GO FOR PRESS PROOF.**

## Press Proof
- [ ] Connect the high pressure K-bottle to GSE.
- [ ] Set the high pressure regulator to 4000 psi.
- [ ] Confirm pressure gauge reading matches regulator setpoint.
- [ ] Connect the low pressure K-bottle to GSE.
- [ ] Set the low pressure regulator to 140 psi.
- [ ] Confirm low pressure gauge reading matches regulator setpoint.
- [ ] Check for leaks at all high and low pressure fittings.
- [ ] Verify all pneumatic hoses are physically connected (coordinate with ACTCOMM).
- [ ] Confirm isolation valves are in correct pre-press configuration.
- [ ] Confirm vent valves are in correct starting configuration.
- [ ] Perform final visual sweep of GSE plumbing.
- [ ] Confirm no tools remain on or near pressurized hardware.
- [ ] **TELL PAD MANAGER GSE IS READY FOR HOTFIRE.**

## Fill
- [ ] Confirm low-pressure and high-pressure regulators are set to the correct fill setpoints (fuel and LOX).
- [ ] Coordinate with Ethanol Fill Operator and LOX Fill Operator to ensure:
  - [ ] Correct GSE lines are being used for each fill.
  - [ ] Vent lines are directed to safe locations.
- [ ] Monitor GSE gauges during Fuel Fill and LOX Fill and call out any off-nominal readings to Operations Manager and Mission Control Manager.
- [ ] Adjust regulators only on request from Operations Manager / Mission Control Manager and confirm changes on gauges.
- [ ] **TELL PAD MANAGER AND OPERATIONS MANAGER WHEN GSE PRESSURE SYSTEMS ARE CONFIGURED FOR HOTFIRE (POST-FILL).**

## Hotfire
- [ ] During pressurization and burn, monitor GSE gauges for any unexpected drops or spikes and report immediately to Operations Manager and Mission Control Manager.
- [ ] Be prepared to close manual GSE valves or vent lines on direction from Operations Manager during an ABORT or safing sequence.
