# Media Checklist

*Role: Drone, cameras, videos, and photos during fire (non-technical coverage only).*

## Equipment Setup
- [ ] Setup photography, drone, and cinematography equipment.
- [ ] Establish designated media operating area outside minimum safe distance.
- [ ] Pre-flight check for all drones (battery, GPS lock, prop integrity, controller link).
- [ ] Position drones before any hazardous operations begin.
- [ ] Confirm redundant batteries and storage media available.
- [ ] **TELL OPERATIONS MANAGER ONCE ALL MEDIA LOCATIONS ARE SECURE AND CLEAR OF HAZARD ZONE.**

## Filming Operations
- [ ] Capture photography and video (non-technical) of the event:
    - [ ] Fluid Fill operations (from safe zone)
    - [ ] Personnel retreat
    - [ ] Pressurization phase
    - [ ] Ignition sequence
    - [ ] Hotfire plume and burn duration
    - [ ] Post-burn venting (from safe distance)
- [ ] Maintain strict situational awareness of all safety announcements from Operations Manager.
- [ ] Immediately land drones if ABORT is called unless otherwise directed.
- [ ] Ensure drone flight paths do not cross critical safety lines or interfere with telemetry/video systems.
- [ ] Maintain visual line of sight with drone at all times.
- [ ] Continue recording until system is declared safe.
- [ ] **BREAK HERE. WAIT FOR OPERATIONS MANAGER TO GIVE ALL-CLEAR AFTER SYSTEM SAFING (SYSTEM BELOW 25 PSI, PER MASTER PROCEDURE).**
