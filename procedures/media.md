# Media Checklist

*Role: Drone, cameras, videos, photos during fire (not technical).*

## Equipment Setup
- [ ] Setup photography, drone, and cinematography equipment.
- [ ] Confirm media operational area is established within a safe distance.
- [ ] Pre-flight check and position drones before hazardous operations begin.
- [ ] **TELL OPERATIONS MANAGER ONCE ALL MEDIA LOCATIONS ARE SECURE AND CLEAR OF HAZARD ZONE.**

## Filming Operations
- [ ] Capture photography and video (non-technical) of the event:
    - [ ] Fluid Fill operations (from safe zone)
    - [ ] Personnel retreat
    - [ ] Ignition and Hotfire operations
- [ ] Maintain strict situational awareness of all safety announcements from Operations Manager.
- [ ] Ensure drone flight paths do not cross critical safety lines.
- [ ] **BREAK HERE. WAIT FOR OPERATIONS MANAGER TO GIVE ALL-CLEAR AFTER TEST SAFING.**
