# GN2/High Press Fill Checklist

*Role: Operates 6k psi k-bottle and high-pressure portion of GSE.*

## GSE High Pressure Setup
- [ ] Position the 6k psi k-bottle safely at the high-pressure GSE station.
- [ ] Connect the high-pressure k-bottle to GSE.
- [ ] Set high-pressure regulator to correct operating parameters (e.g., **4500 psi** per procedure).
- [ ] **TELL GSE MANAGER HIGH PRESSURE K-BOTTLE IS SET.**

## Operational Phases
- [ ] **BREAK HERE. WAIT FOR OPERATIONS MANAGER TO GIVE THE GO TO BEGIN FLUID/GAS PREP.**
- [ ] Continuously monitor pressures on the high-pressure section of the GSE throughout fueling operations.
- [ ] **TELL GSE MANAGER/MISSION CONTROL OF ANY PRESSURE SUPPLY ISSUES OR LINE DROPS.**

## Depressurization & Safing
- [ ] Close main valve on the 6k psi k-bottle once safing command is received and tanks are being vented.
- [ ] Assist GSE Manager in depressurizing regulators through manual hose vent valves to remove stored line pressure.
- [ ] **TELL GSE MANAGER HIGH PRESSURE SYSTEM IS DEPRESSURIZED AND SAFE.**
